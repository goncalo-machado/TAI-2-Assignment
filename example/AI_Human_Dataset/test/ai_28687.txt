It gives more power to the votes of people in less populated states, and it has resulted in the election of two Presidents who lost the popular vote.In 2000, George W. Bush won the Electoral College, but lost the popular vote to Al Gore. In 2016, Donald Trump won the Electoral College, but lost the popular vote to Hillary Clinton. These two elections have shown that the Electoral College is a system that is not fair to the majority of Americans.The Electoral College is also a system that is not transparent. There is no way to know for sure how individual electors are going to vote. This lack of transparency makes it possible for electors to be influenced by special interests, and it undermines the publics confidence in the election process.I believe that the best way to ensure that the President of the United States is elected by the majority of the people is to abolish the Electoral College and elect the President by popular vote.
