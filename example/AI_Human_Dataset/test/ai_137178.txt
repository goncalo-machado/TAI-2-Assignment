Our society today often tends to favor conformity over individuality due to certain social pressures that limit creative thinking and encourage sameness. This can be seen in the day people think, dress, and act, opting for the safety of conformity and the group mindset in order to fit in. As the essay mentions, “Conformity facilitates consensus and thus generates security and stability within a society” (Goodman, 2018). People prefer certainty and will often limit their independent thinking in order to blend in. This mentality can also be found in the job market, their having unique skills or ideas are often not valued and may even be seen as a disadvantage. People are often judged harshly for expressing their on thoughts or ideas that deviate from that is considered “normal”. In conclusion, in our society today, conformity is often favored over individuality.
