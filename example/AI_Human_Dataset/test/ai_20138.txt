Once you have researched how to gain confidence in order to change a first impression, it is important to look within yourself and reach a clear vision of how you would like to be perceived; this will serve as the bedrock for your journey. After reaching this point, it is time to formulate a plan of action. What steps are necessary to help you work hard to reach your desired goal? However, it is also important to identify the potential challenges one may face when attempting to change a first impression. Come up with possible solutions to problems you may encounter and develop a strategy to help you combat them. Finally, activities like JROTC can act as an excellent opportunity to gain the necessary skills to create a successful plan and therefore make the necessary changes to how you are initially perceived.
