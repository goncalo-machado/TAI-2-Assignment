This is crucial because it means that candidates have to campaign and focus their efforts on multiple states rather than just focusing on heavily populated areas. This leads to a more inclusive and representative election, where candidates have to take into account the concerns and priorities of voters from all over the country. In contrast, a direct popular vote system would likely lead to candidates only campaigning in heavily populated areas, neglecting the concerns of voters in rural and smaller states.Another significant advantage of the Electoral College is that it provides a more stable and predictable outcome.
